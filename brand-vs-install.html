Why brand names matter less than installation

Two homes can buy the same brand and get very different results. The difference is design, airflow, and verification.

Plain-language explanation

Comfort depends on correct sizing, duct design, airflow, and charge. A premium system installed poorly will still short cycle, run loud, and waste energy.
